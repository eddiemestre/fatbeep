HOME

Collecting and sharing art while making friends on the way. This is a place for people who think a "story" is more than just a snapchat of a friend at the beach. This is a safe space for artists to share what interests them without the added bullshit of distribution and live shows or galleries or whatever.

Point is if you like it we like it. As long as you aren't offending someone (no bigotry here but we enjoy art that challenges us) lets throw it up here and give it space of its own.

Lots of things in the works. Things are bound to get crazy before long.

Look for us where you least expect us. Lets try to make this shit a little more interesting.

-Fat Beep
